Counselling and cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) in Kentish Town, London NW5

Help for any matters including anxiety, depression, stress, relationships and addiction.

Based in NW5, I am a Cognitive Behavioural Therapist offering counselling to individuals. I offer a safe, comfortable and confidential environment. 

Whilst I follow the cognitive behavioural traditions of therapy, I also employ helpful techniques from other forms of therapy designed to best suit your needs.

Qualifications : SCOTACS Diploma in Counselling and Groupwork, COSCA Certificate in Counselling Skills, SCOTACS Certificate in Advanced Clinical Skills, Diploma in Training and Development.

Specialisations : I have sound experience of working with Anxiety, Depression, Stress and Addiction. I also help people to identify patterns of behaviour that are helpful or cause difficulties in areas such as relationships and careers.

Methodology : Integrative Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, borrowing from other therapeutic models.

Experience: I have worked as a CBT psychotherapist for the National Health Service in the UK and in private practice. Previously, I was an Addictions Counsellor for an addictions service in Glasgow, which included working with Criminal Justice Social Work. I have also delivered training in CBT and counselling at The Centre of Therapy, Glasgow.
