Frequently asked questions

What would I expect?

Firstly, you're the boss. You tailor our meetings to talk about the things you want to. We will then work together to structure the sessions to help you to get where you want to be. Sessions last for 50 minutes.

In the first session, we will need to run through what we should both expect at our meetings, this allows us to stay safe. It will also be necessary to provide some personal details (which will be kept separate from any other records) and what you want to get from therapy. This lets me get to know you. We can go through this together at our first session, or if you prefer, I can send you a form or two to complete before you arrive at our initial meeting.

What do you require from me?

You simply need to bring yourself and be honest.

In session we can work through techniques and exercises to help you with your thoughts, feelings and behaviour. You can use these techniques on a daily basis. Each time we reconvene, we will review what you noticed by using these techniques. You will also be encouraged to put ypur changes into practice between sessions. It is up to you as to how much of this you wish to do, but by doing so, what have you got to lose?

Who needs to know?

What is brought to session is dealt with in confidence. However, this confidence cannot extend to matters which may indicate harm to yourself or another.
